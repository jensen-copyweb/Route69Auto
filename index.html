What We Do

OUR SERVICES

From diagnostics to full rebuilds, we handle both the electrical and mechanical side of any vehicle or machine.

Auto-Electrical

Complete auto-electrical diagnostics, wiring, alternators, starters, and full rewiring services.

Mechanical Repairs

Engine overhauls, gearbox repairs, suspension work, brake systems, and full mechanical maintenance.

Diagnostics

Advanced computer diagnostics for fault finding, ECU programming, and system analysis.

Aircon Service

Air conditioning regas, leak detection, compressor replacement, and full climate system repairs.

Earthmoving Vehicles

Full service and repair for earthmoving equipment — excavators, loaders, dozers, and graders.

Diesel & Petrol Motors

Specialist diesel and petrol engine services, injector cleaning, turbo repairs, and fuel system work.

Roadworthy

Roadworthy inspections and repairs to get your vehicle compliant and back on the road.

Hydraulics

Hydraulic hose repairs, cylinder rebuilds, pump services, and complete hydraulic system diagnostics.

Trailer Repairs

Trailer wiring, lighting, brake systems, suspension repairs, and full trailer maintenance.

Tractor Services

Full tractor maintenance, engine rebuilds, PTO repairs, and agricultural equipment servicing.

Generator Services

Generator installation, maintenance, load testing, and emergency repair services.

Undercarriage & Small Plant

Undercarriage inspections, track adjustments, roller replacements, and small plant equipment servicing.
